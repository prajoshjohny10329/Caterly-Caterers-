About Us

Chiramel Luxury Auditorium
Enjoy a Luxury Experience

"Welcome to Chiramel Auditorium in Udayanapuram, Vaikom – the premier venue for weddings, parties, receptions, cultural events, performing arts, and social gatherings! Strategically situated opposite the Udayanapuram Homeopathy Hospital and just 1km from the renowned Udayanapuram Temple, our auditorium offers unparalleled accessibility within the city and throughout the state."

"Established in 2017, Chiramel Auditorium stands out as one of the largest centrally air-conditioned auditoriums in Udayanapuram, Vaikom. Whether you're planning a grand wedding celebration, an elegant art exhibition, a serene spiritual class, an informative seminar, a professional business conference, or an impactful product launch, Chiramel Auditorium provides the perfect setting for your event.

"Our state-of-the-art facilities and attentive staff ensure that every aspect of your event is executed flawlessly, leaving you and your guests with unforgettable memories. Discover why Chiramel Auditorium is the preferred choice for discerning hosts and event organizers in Vaikom."
